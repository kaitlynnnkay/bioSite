THE NEBRASKA BLUES

Nick & The Nebraska Blues

Nick first became interested in English football while watching the 2008 Champion’s League final. It was Chelsea Football Club versus Manchester United and Nick instantly became a fan when star player Didier Drogba slapped a Man United player during an argument on the pitch.


Many years and Chelsea trophies later, Nick now represents the Omaha chapter of the Chelsea in America organization. In this prestigious role, he is responsible for organizing match-day gatherings, running the Nebraska Blues Twitter account, and trying to increase membership numbers.


Details Follow Nick and the Nebraska Blues at @NebraskaBlues on Twitter!

Unlike American sports, Chelsea matches air in primetime for London, meaning Nick and other Chelsea fans are gathering at the local football bar between the hours of 6am and 9am most weekends to catch the games. And for a chance to see the club in person, travel will always be required. His love for the team has taken him to Florida for a pre-season exhibition match against Arsenal, and to London twice, though only to visit the club grounds and stadium. His support of the team has even gotten him a percentage ownership in the club’s stadium, Stamford Bridge, which is entirely supporter-owned.
