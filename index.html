The Life of Nicholas Dawson

About

Born August 9, 1991, Nicholas Dawson has lived many chapters in his almost 34 years of life. From growing up in D.C., to his adolescence in the midwest, to being an enlisted Airmen, and now a husband and first-time father, Nick's path continues to take him to new places.

Hobbies

A man of varying interests, Nick Dawson routinely engages in worldwide travel, tiki culture, and all major (and minor) sports fandoms. His most time-consuming hobby, though, is his role as the Steward of the Nebraska Blues chapter of the Chelsea in America organization.
